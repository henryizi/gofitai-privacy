GoFitAI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2025

1. Introduction

GoFitAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our AI-powered fitness and nutrition application.

This policy complies with Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO) and follows the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data (PCPD) guidelines for AI and health data processing.

2. Data Controller Information

Data Controller: GoFitAI Development Team
Contact: privacy@gofitai.com
Data Protection Officer: dpo@gofitai.com

3. Personal Data We Collect

3.1 Account Information

  • Full name and preferred display name
  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Account credentials (securely hashed passwords)
  • Account preferences and settings

3.2 Health & Biometric Data

  • Physical measurements: height, weight, body fat percentage
  • Personal characteristics: age/birthday, gender
  • Fitness information: training level, primary goals, activity level
  • Body composition photos (front and back view)
  • Workout history and exercise performance data
  • Progress tracking measurements and achievements

3.3 Nutrition & Dietary Data

  • Food photos for AI nutritional analysis
  • Meal logs and calorie tracking
  • Dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Nutrition goals and target macronutrients

3.4 Usage & Analytics Data

  • App usage patterns and feature interaction
  • Device information (model, OS version, app version)
  • Performance metrics and error logs
  • User feedback and support interactions

4. How We Use Your Data

4.1 Primary Services

  • Generate personalized workout plans based on your goals and fitness level
  • Create customized nutrition plans and meal recommendations
  • Provide AI-powered body composition and aesthetic analysis
  • Track your fitness progress and provide achievement insights
  • Deliver personalized health and fitness coaching

4.2 AI Processing

  • Analyze body photos using computer vision for aesthetic assessment
  • Process food images to identify nutrition content and calories
  • Generate recommendations using machine learning algorithms
  • Improve AI model accuracy through anonymized data analysis

4.3 Service Improvement

  • Enhance app functionality and user experience
  • Develop new features and improve existing ones
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
  • Conduct research to improve health outcomes

5. AI Data Processing & PCPD Compliance

We follow PCPD AI guidance for all automated processing of your personal data.

5.1 Automated Decision Making

Our AI systems make automated decisions about:

  • Workout plan difficulty and exercise selection
  • Nutrition recommendations and calorie targets
  • Body composition assessments and ratings
  • Progress tracking and achievement recognition

5.2 Human Oversight & Rights

  • You can request human review of any AI-generated recommendations
  • You can opt-out of automated processing where technically feasible
  • We provide explanations for AI decisions upon request
  • Regular human monitoring ensures AI fairness and accuracy

5.3 AI Risk Controls

  • Regular bias testing and fairness assessments
  • Data minimization - only necessary data is processed
  • Accuracy monitoring and error correction procedures
  • Secure AI model deployment with privacy protections

6. Data Storage & Security

6.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Primary storage: Supabase cloud infrastructure (secure, encrypted)
  • Photo storage: Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Geographic location: Data centers with appropriate security certifications
  • Backup storage: Encrypted backups in geographically separate locations

6.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive health data
  • Multi-factor authentication and secure access controls
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee access controls and privacy training
  • Incident detection and response procedures

6.3 Data Retention

  • Active account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Backup data: 12 months after account deletion
  • Analytics data: Anonymized and retained for service improvement
  • Legal obligations: Retained as required by applicable laws

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data only in these limited circumstances:

7.1 Service Providers

  • Cloud infrastructure providers (Supabase) for data storage
  • AI processing services (DeepSeek, Gemini) for analysis
  • Analytics providers for app performance monitoring
  • Support service providers for customer assistance

7.2 Legal Requirements

  • Court orders, legal processes, or regulatory requests
  • Protection of rights, property, or safety
  • Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

7.3 Business Transfers

  • Mergers, acquisitions, or asset transfers (with continued privacy protection)

8. Your Rights Under PDPO

Under Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, you have the following rights:

8.1 Access Rights

  • Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • View how your data is being processed and used
  • Understand the purposes of data collection

8.2 Correction Rights

  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Request correction of AI-generated assessments

8.3 Deletion Rights

  • Delete your account and all associated personal data
  • Request removal of specific data categories
  • Data portability before deletion

8.4 Additional Rights

  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Object to automated decision-making
  • Data portability to other services
  • Lodge complaints with the Privacy Commissioner

8.5 How to Exercise Your Rights

You can exercise these rights through:

  • App Settings → Privacy & Security → Data Management
  • Email us at privacy@gofitai.com
  • In-app support chat or help center

We will respond to your requests within 40 days as required by PDPO.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred outside Hong Kong for processing by our AI service providers. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Contractual safeguards and data processing agreements
  • Adequacy decisions and approved transfer mechanisms
  • Regular monitoring of international service providers

10. Children's Privacy

GoFitAI is not intended for users under 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately and terminate the account.

11. Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  • We will notify the Privacy Commissioner within 72 hours
  • Affected users will be notified without undue delay
  • We will provide clear information about the breach and protective measures
  • We will offer appropriate support and remediation

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will:

  • Notify you of material changes through the app or email
  • Post the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Obtain new consent if required by law
  • Provide transition periods for policy changes
